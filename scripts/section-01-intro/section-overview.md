## Overview
- **Domain**: Course orientation (no exam domain)
- **Exam Weight**: N/A
- **Lecture Count**: 1 lecture (combined)

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this section, students will be able to:
- Identify the exam format, scaled scoring model, and the 720/1000 passing threshold.
- Distinguish the five exam domains and recall their relative weights (Domain 1 at 27% as the highest-leverage area).
- Recognize the six possible exam scenarios and predict that any given exam will surface four of them.
- Apply a study strategy appropriate to their available preparation window (next-day cram vs. two-week study plan).
- Navigate the official Anthropic exam guide to locate domain task statements, sample questions, and preparation exercises.
