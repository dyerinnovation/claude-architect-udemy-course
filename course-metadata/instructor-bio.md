## Profile photo spec

- **Framing:** headshot, chest-up, square crop, eyes on the upper third — Udemy minimum 200×200, shoot at 1000×1000 for safety.
- **Lighting:** natural window light, soft key from camera-left, neutral mid-grey or off-white background — no studio gradient, no office clutter.
- **Wardrobe:** practitioner — solid button-down (charcoal, navy, or olive) or plain dark tee. No logos, no jackets with branded hardware, no lanyards.
- **Mood:** direct eye contact, relaxed jaw, mouth closed or slight press — approachable but not grinning. Shoulders squared to camera, not angled.
- **Post:** minimal retouching, no filters, no vignette, no branded overlay on the photo itself (the course image handles branding).
